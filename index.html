HACK AND TACKLE 1.0

Hack and Tackle 1.0 is a 24 hours hackathon that will be based on five different tracks namely, environment, healthcare, womenâ€™s safety, agriculture and social welfare. Here, the participants can choose the problem statement given by industries and give the solution or they can come up with the ideas to solve their own problems related to the tracks and build working solutions for the same.

Who can participate?

Students belonging to any year, pursuing their undergraduate or postgraduate programmes, in academic institutions across India.

How can one register for Hack and Tackle 1.0?

Hack and Tackle takes place in two phases. In Phase 1, students can participate by completing their registration and submitting the solutions of the given problem, or their own problems and solutions to one of the tracks of their choice. Registrations for both Phases have to be completed online. If Shortlisted for Phase 2, the participants will be informed to pay the registration fee (INR 2000 + INR 360 (18% GST) per team) for participating in Phase 2. This will happen at SSN College of Engineering, Kalavakkam, Chennai - 603 110.

Why Sponsor?

Hack and Tackle 1.0 will be the forum to provide an opportunity for industry experts to connect with the student community across India.
